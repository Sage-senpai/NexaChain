# How to Create Your Video Thumbnail

## Quick Option: Screenshot from Video

1. Open your video file (`nexachain-intro.mp4`) in any video player
2. Pause at an engaging frame (preferably with text or logo visible)
3. Take a screenshot
4. Save as `nexachain-thumbnail.jpg` in this folder

## Professional Option: Design Custom Thumbnail

### Recommended Dimensions
- **Size**: 1920 x 1080 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **Format**: JPG (high quality, 85-95% compression)

### Design Tips
1. Include the Nexachain logo
2. Use your brand colors (gold #D4AF37)
3. Add engaging text like:
   - "Discover Nexachain"
   - "Your Investment Partner"
   - "Watch Our Story"
4. Keep it clean and professional
5. Ensure text is readable even when small

### Free Tools to Create Thumbnails
- **Canva**: https://canva.com (easiest, templates available)
- **Figma**: https://figma.com (professional, free)
- **GIMP**: https://gimp.org (desktop app, like Photoshop)
- **Photopea**: https://photopea.com (online Photoshop alternative)

### Canva Quick Start
1. Go to Canva.com and sign up (free)
2. Create new design → Custom size → 1920 x 1080
3. Choose a template or start blank
4. Add your Nexachain branding
5. Download as JPG
6. Save as `nexachain-thumbnail.jpg`

## What Happens Without a Thumbnail?

If you don't add a thumbnail, the video player will show:
- A black screen before the video plays
- Or the first frame of your video (browser default)

Adding a custom thumbnail makes your video look much more professional and engaging!
